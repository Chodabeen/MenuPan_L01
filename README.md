# MenuPan_L01
# L01 - 메뉴판 저장하기
[ArrayList 사용]

다음과 같이 메뉴에 따라 데이터를 입력받거나, 삭제하거나, 리스트를 보여주는 프로그램을 객체지향으로 제 작하라.(클래스이름, 멤버변수, 메소드 및 파라미터, 리턴타입은 각자 결정함) 

실행 예시
```
1) add, 2) modify, 3) remove, 4) list, 5) total number, 6) exit > 1
Enter name, origin, price > pig kor 9000
1) add, 2) modify, 3) remove, 4) list, 5) total number, 6) exit > 1
Enter name, origin, price > rib aus 15000
1) add, 2) modify, 3) remove, 4) list, 5) total number, 6) exit > 4
================================================
num     name            origin          price
1       pig             kor             9000
2       rib             aus             15000
1) add, 2) modify, 3) remove, 4) list, 5) total number, 6) exit > 5
Number of registered menu is 2
1) add, 2) modify, 3) remove, 4) list, 5) total number, 6) exit > 3
================================================
num     name            origin          price
1       pig             kor             9000
2       rib             aus             15000
Enter a number to remove > 1
Removed
1) add, 2) modify, 3) remove, 4) list, 5) total number, 6) exit > 2
================================================
num     name            origin          price
1       rib             aus             15000
Enter a number to modify > 1
Enter name, origin, price > rib aus 12000
1) add, 2) modify, 3) remove, 4) list, 5) total number, 6) exit > 6
Ended
```
